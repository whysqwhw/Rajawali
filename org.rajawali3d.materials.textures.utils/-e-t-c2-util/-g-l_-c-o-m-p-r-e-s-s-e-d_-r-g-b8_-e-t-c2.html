static val GL_COMPRESSED_RGB8_ETC2: Int

The main difference is that the newer version contains three new modes; the ‘T-mode’ and the ‘H-mode’ which are good for sharp chrominance blocks and the ‘Planar’ mode which is good for smooth blocks. NOTE: ETC1 files can be passed to ETC2 decoders as GLES30#GL_COMPRESSED_RGB8_ETC2.
