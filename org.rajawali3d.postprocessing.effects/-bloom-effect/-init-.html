BloomEffect(scene: Scene, camera: Camera, width: Int, height: Int, lowerThreshold: Int, upperThreshold: Int, blendMode: BlendMode)

Bloom or glow is used to amplify light in a scene. It produces light bleeding. Bright light will extend to other parts of the scene. The colors that will bleed can be controlled by specifying the lower and upper threshold colors.
